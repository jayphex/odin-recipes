Description

This pancake recipe will make your mornings brighter, it certainly does whenever I make mine!

Quick and easy with a prep time of 10 minutes and cooking time of 5!

  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 2 cups of almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix dry recipes into a bowl
  2. Crack the eggs and mix it into the bowl
  3. Pour milk and mix it in the bowl
  4. Heat a skillet to medium temperature using butter or oil
  5. Fry the pancakes!
